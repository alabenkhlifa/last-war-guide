Free-to-Play
Daily Routine

In the challenging world of Last War: Survival, every move counts—especially for free-to-play players.

This guide is designed to help you make the most of your time and resources by outlining the essential daily tasks you should focus on.

By following this routine, you’ll optimize your progress and stay competitive without spending a dime.

Let’s dive into the strategies that will keep you ahead in the game!

Arms Race

Every day, you should participate in the Arms and get the last chest as shown in the picture.
To do so, you have a get the last chest of 3 different races.
Personally, I usually go for Drone Boost, Hero Advancement and Unit Progression.
If you miss any of them then you should compensate with other races like City Building or Tech Research.

TIP: You can go for the second chest for these 2 races instead of going for the third chest on one of them.
