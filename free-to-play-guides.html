Free-to-Play
Daily Routine

In the challenging world of Last War: Survival, every move counts—especially for free-to-play players.

This guide is designed to help you make the most of your time and resources by outlining the essential daily tasks you should focus on.

By following this routine, you’ll optimize your progress and stay competitive without spending a dime.

Let’s dive into the strategies that will keep you ahead in the game!

Arms Race

Every day, you should participate in the Arms and get the last chest as shown in the picture.
To do so, you have a get the last chest of 3 different races.
Personally, I usually go for Drone Boost, Hero Advancement and Unit Progression.
If you miss any of them then you should compensate with other races like City Building or Tech Research.

TIP: You can go for the second chest for these 2 races instead of going for the third chest on one of them.
TIP: Don't spend drone points on the Drone Boost race, instead use stamina. (checkout Stamina section below)

Special Events

Boss Rampage Event

Every 2 week on Sunday at 0:00, 6:00, and 12:00 server time, a Rampage Boss will awaken from the tombs.
Commanders with Base level 8 and above can attack the Rampage Boss, with a maximum of 3 attacks per boss.
In this special event, 3 different Bosses spawns in the middle of the map.
The whole server have 24 hours to kill them.
You get rewards based on how much damage was dealt to the bosses after the server resets.
Rewards will be sent to your mailbox.

Every Boss is vulnerable against a specific type of squad, as listed below :

  1. The first Boss (Code 87) takes +50% more damage from Tank Heroes.
  2. The second Boss (Code 39) takes +50% more damage from Aircraft Heroes.
  3. The third Boss (Code 64) takes +50% more damage from Missile Heroes.

Some Tips:

  • Align your squad to Maximize damage.
  • Don't forget to scout before attacking to get +1% extra damage.
  • Don't use Defenders in your squad, only use Damage Dealers.
  • Only hit with your Main Squad.
  • Try to make it to TOP 100 to receive rewards.
To get FREE GEMS, click on any Boss, then Ranking Icon, like the TOP 3 damage dealer.
